My Professional Experience

Although it is very early in my career and I still do not have much professional experience, I have had two very influential jobs that have helped me grow as a professional.

Manager – Parsons Family Farmstand

For two summers, I worked at the Parsons Family Farmstand as a manager. Some of my responsibilities included:

  • In charge of overseeing and facilitating daily tasks
  • Developing and maintaining relationships with customers and suppliers
  • Raised performance in areas of sales, management, and operations

Counselor – Forman School

For two summers during the month of July, I worked at a summer camp for children with learning disabilities. Some of my responsibilities included:

  • Offered guidance and advice to help cultivate self-esteem and community involvement
  • Communicated closely with upper-level management to devise programs and activities
  • Identified risk and resiliency factors within individual youth and developed intervention strategies
  • Provided individual counseling services for students with adjustment issues
